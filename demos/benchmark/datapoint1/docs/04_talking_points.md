# Talking Points

## Data Point 1 — Pre-Index Optimization

### What it is
Pre-index optimization is the stage where text is evaluated before embedding.

### Why it matters
This is the last point where text is:
- cheap to process
- fully interpretable
- easy to control deterministically

### What Nol8 does
- keep useful content
- mask sensitive content
- drop low-value content
- route content that should not be embedded

### Business value
- lower embedding cost
- lower storage growth
- cleaner retrieval
- lower compliance exposure

### Executive sound bites
- "We reduce what becomes embeddings."
- "We control the data path before cost compounds."
- "We improve the quality of what enters the AI system."

### Technical sound bites
- "A chunk is still text here, not a vector."
- "Once embedded, cost has already been incurred."
- "Deterministic control belongs before embedding."

### Red flags to avoid
- do not claim Nol8 is the RAG system
- do not claim Nol8 replaces the model
- do not claim Nol8 makes the model smarter
